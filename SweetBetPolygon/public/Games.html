How To Work

This is a fair game peer to peer in an intelligent contract to multiply your Matic. Simply choose the size of the bet and play against other players, depending on the game you choose. Only one of you will be the unfortunate and the rest will win!

The game allows you to multiply your bets by playing against other players. When placing the bet, enter the group of other players who have made the same bet. To place the bet, send the amount of the bet to the address of the contract. If you send more, the contract will return the change. As soon as the number of players in your group reaches the maximum, the draw begins!

Upon entering the group, each player places a piece of candy in the donut. At the time of the draw, the donut will start spinning and when it stops, Homer will bite into the donut and indicate the unlucky player of the round. All those who are lucky will receive their bet + X% of gain according to each type of game.

If you get scared long before the draw, you can leave the game. To cancel your bet, you must click on the cancel bet button. You cancel one of your bets per transaction. If you have multiple bets, repeat the procedure up to the number of bets placed. Attention! Only 99% of your bet is returned.

FAQ

What is the probability that he wins?

The possibilities of winning are: 80% in the strawberry donut. 75% on the chocolate donut. 66% in the vanilla donut and 50% in the mixed donut. Consequently, the probability of losing is less than 20%, 25%, 34% and 50% respectively. At the same time, even if you are the unlucky one, you still have the chance to win the jackpot (2% for the half of the jackpot, 0.2% for the jackpot), which can make you a real winner. Important! How is the unlucky player selected?

The selection is made randomly from an unknown hash at the time of the draw, and there is no real way to influence the outcome either by the players or by the developers. Developers are engaged in marketing and technical support only. Developers can not influence game results.

What is the guarantee that I will receive what I won playing?

The game smart contract is deployed int Polygon blockchain. It is absolutely and fully automated. No administrative actions are required to make payouts. At all! The contract does not even have administrative functions. The contract creator does not have access to the code and data. The contract can not be changed or deleted. Funds sent on contract balance are distributed among participants, strictly following rules described therein, and cannot be withdrawn by any third party.
